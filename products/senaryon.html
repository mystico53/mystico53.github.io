Senaryon Product Page

Introduction

Welcome to Senaryon, a groundbreaking game engine I spearheaded at planpolitik, the leader in civic education in Europe. This innovative platform is the cornerstone of our transition to digital learning, enabling interactive, web-based workshops that have fundamentally changed how we teach and engage with students across the globe.

My Role

As the architect of planpolitik's Online Education Department, I had the unique opportunity to build and lead a department from the ground up. My vision for digitizing collaborative learning formats was not just about keeping pace with technology—it was about leading the charge, particularly as we navigated the challenges of the pandemic.

Challenges & Solutions

The digital landscape posed a significant challenge: how do we replicate the richness of face-to-face learning in an online environment? The answer lay in the development of Senaryon. Through this platform, we've successfully digitized our company's signature teaching methods, enabling educators and students to create customized learning experiences.

Outcome & Impact

The launch of over 30 digital teaching tools, under my guidance, has positioned planpolitik at the forefront of online civic education. Senaryon alone has impacted 50,000 students in 1,000 schools and 150 universities. Our efforts have been recognized with two international and four national awards, affirming our commitment to educational innovation.

Learnings

Each step in the development of Senaryon has been a learning opportunity. Embracing agile methodologies proved crucial, as did fostering a culture of problem-based learning. The collaboration across departments was not just beneficial—it was essential for the seamless integration of new features.

Technologies & Tools Used

The backbone of Senaryon's success was a suite of cutting-edge technologies and a commitment to agile project management. Our toolbox ranged from the latest in collaborative platforms to innovative project tracking software, all of which played a pivotal role in bringing our digital teaching methods to life.

Team & Leadership

None of this would have been possible without the talented team of 12 I recruited, including engineers, web designers, instructional designers, and QA experts. Managing a $2 million budget, I led the department in setting objectives, maintaining product roadmaps, and implementing company-wide processes that ensured a collaborative and efficient workflow.
