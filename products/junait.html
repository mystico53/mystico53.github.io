Junait Product Page

Introduction

Introducing Junait, an innovative educational tool that I have had the pleasure of bringing to life. Designed to look and function like a social network, Junait is an interactive platform that educates elementary school children in German-speaking regions on digital literacy and internet safety.

Concept and Funding

The journey of Junait began with a vision to create a safe digital environment for children to learn about the perils of social media. My commitment to this cause was bolstered by securing $400,000 in governmental funding, which set the stage for Junait's development.

Team and Expertise

Building Junait was a collaborative effort. I brought on board a skilled web developer, a creative product designer, and a talented game designer. We also tapped into the insights of internet security experts and partnered with Thomas Feibel, a leading authority in digital literacy for children, to craft an engaging narrative.

Game Mechanics and Development

The mechanics of Junait were carefully wireframed to ensure an immersive experience. As the product manager, I steered the daily operations of a cross-functional team to turn these blueprints into reality. Our collective efforts were focused on designing interactive tasks that not only educate but also empower students to take charge of their online security.

Testing and Outreach

Before launch, Junait was rigorously tested with over 500 students, providing invaluable feedback that shaped the final product. I took the lead in creating compelling copy for the marketing website, ensuring that Junait's message was heard loud and clear.

Daily Use and Recognition

Today, Junait stands as a testament to innovation in education, with teachers incorporating it into daily lessons to promote awareness of online threats. The platform's success has been acknowledged with two national awards for its contribution to increasing online security.
